Industrial Automation

SPM with Pneumatic Application

Custom-engineered solutions to automate complex assembly and testing operations.

We design and fabricate Special Purpose Machines (SPMs) tailored to specific customer requirements. By integrating advanced pneumatic logic with precision mechanics, our machines significantly reduce manual effort, improve cycle times, and ensure consistent quality in assembly lines.

PLC Controlled Pneumatic Logic

Key System Features

Our SPMs are built for durability and ease of operation.

⚙
Pneumatic Actuation High-speed cylinders for clamping & pressing.
⚡
PLC / Logic Control Automated sequencing for error-proofing.
🛠
Ergonomic Design Operator-friendly workstations.

Typical Applications

Assembly Pressing

Pneumatic press stations for bearing, bush, or pin insertion with force monitoring.

Leak Testing

Automated fixtures to check seal integrity using air pressure decay methods.

Drilling/Tapping

Multi-spindle heads for simultaneous drilling operations on mass-produced parts.
