Product Overview

Continuity Testing & Assembly Holders

Our specialized testing fixtures and holder assemblies are designed to streamline the wiring harness manufacturing process. These jigs facilitate reliable electrical connectivity checks between terminals and ensure precise terminal insertion into coupler connectors.

  • Error-proofing for complex wiring harnesses.
  • Custom-machined for specific connector profiles.
  • Durable Nylon/Delrin construction for long life.
