Operational Strength

Facility Infrastructure

Beyond machinery, we have invested in a robust supporting ecosystem to ensure zero downtime and consistent production quality.

100% Power Backup

Equipped with a 62.5 KVA Silent DG Set to ensure 24/7 uninterrupted production, eliminating delivery delays caused by power outages.

In-House Tool Room

Dedicated facility for designing and manufacturing Jigs, Fixtures, and Gauges, significantly reducing lead time for new product development.

CAD/CAM Design

Advanced workstations running SolidWorks and AutoCAD for precise 3D modeling, drafting, and tool path generation before machining begins.

Preventive Maintenance

Strict adherence to TPM (Total Productive Maintenance) schedules ensures machine accuracy is maintained year-round and breakdowns are minimized.
